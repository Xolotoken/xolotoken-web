Goal

Make donations through blockchain technology to help non-profit organizations.

Specific objectives

  • Donate 6% of the total supply to two fundations chosen by the project team through the XOLO TOKEN exchange to cover part of their budget.
  • Incentivize with 3% of each transaction by redistributing token to all holders to obtain profits at every moment.
  • Send 3% of liquidity funds to the pancakeswap pool in each transaction carried out to maintain the stability and liquidity of the project.
  • 2% of each transaction will automatically go to the operative area to continue improving the project.
  • Delevop an NFT market ecosystem through blockchain to have a community of at least 500,000 people.
