Cómo unirse a la venta privada:


  • Compre o transfiera BNB a su cuenta P2P (sin tarifa de transferencia) en el segmento Binance P2P.

  • Una vez tenga fondos en su cuenta P2P envíe BNB (sin tarifa de envío) a la cuenta oficial de XOLO TOKEN de Binance: payments@xolotoken.org

NOTA: Si envía un pago menor a 0.2 BNB se considerará como donación al proyecto y no se devolverá.

Goal

Make donations through blockchain technology to help non-profit organizations.

Specific objectives

  • Donate 6% of the total supply to two foundations chosen by the project team through the XOLO TOKEN exchange to cover part of their budget.
  • Incentivize with 3% of each transaction by redistributing tokens to all holders to obtain profits for each minute.
  • Send 3% of liquidity funds to the pancakeswap pool for each transaction, out to maintain the stability and liquidity of the project.
  • Send 2% of each transaction automatically to the wallet of the foundation chosen by the community helping it to cover part of its operating expenses.
  • Delevop an NFT market ecosystem through blockchain to have a community of at least 500,000 people.
